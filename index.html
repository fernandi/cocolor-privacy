ARTICLE 3 : Description and access to the services

Cocolor is a color playing app with two first games, Am I blue? and Tea for Two.

Am I Blue? : A color building challenge  

The goal of the game is to make the little guy in the drawing disappear by mixing three primary pigments to match the background color.  To do this you gradually add cyan, yellow and magenta as you attempt to get ever closer to the target color. Take your time, because there’s no going back when  your color mixture gets too dark!

Will you be able to match six colors in a row? Or would you rather just relax and create a series of Andy Warhol style portraits?  

 

 

Tea for Two : A dueling tea party

Tea for Two is played by two people. Challenge yourself to match the background color on your side before your opponent matches theirs.  

In this game, you can add and also subtract primary pigments if you go too far.  If your opponent wins, you offer them a cup of tea. The first player to match 3 colors wins the round, with all their colors shaping the final landscape.
